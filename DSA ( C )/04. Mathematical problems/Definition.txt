### b) GCD & LCM

Concepts:

 Greatest Common Divisor (GCD)
 Least Common Multiple (LCM)
 Euclidean Algorithm

Common problems:

 Find GCD of two numbers
 GCD of an array

Why important:

 Used in fractions, ratios, and number reduction problems

Concepts:

 Finding all divisors of a number
 Counting divisors efficiently
 Sum of divisors

Common problems:

 Print all factors

Concepts:

 Extract digits using modulo and division
 Reverse a number
 Count digits

Common problems:

 Reverse a number

 Sum of digits
 Product of digits

Why important:

 Frequently asked in entry-level interviews

## 4.3 Modular Arithmetic

Very important for competitive programming.

Concepts:

 Modulo operator (`%`)
 Handling large numbers
 Modular addition, subtraction, multiplication

Common problems:

 Large factorial modulo M
 Power modulo (fast exponentiation)
 Preventing integer overflow

Why important:

 Used when numbers exceed data type limits

## 4.4 Bitwise Mathematics

This connects math with bit manipulation.

Concepts:

 Binary representation of numbers
 AND, OR, XOR, NOT
 Left shift (`<<`) and Right shift (`>>`)

Common problems:

 Check odd or even using bits
 Count set bits
 Find unique element in array
 Power of two check

Why important:

 Extremely fast operations
 Common in low-level optimizations
